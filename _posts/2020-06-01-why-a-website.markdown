Hello internet.

Let me explain the purpose of this website.

Over the last couple of years, I've gained experience in coding (mainly data science and Bioinformatics projects in Python). My first reason for creating a website is to gain experience in creating websites (very meta, I know). 

My second and main reason is a change in philosophy from consuming to producing. I've been functioning as a human academic sponge for quite some time. As my university studies are coming to an end, I want to focus more on putting out interesting content, instead of just reading and keeping to myself.

Putting out content does not necessarily mean there is a desire for me to reach a big audience. You can consider this site a big [rubber-ducking](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=Rubber%20ducking) experiment for all things science, code and more.
